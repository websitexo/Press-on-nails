Size Guide

To make sure your press-on nails fit perfectly, please measure your natural nails using a UK coin. Take a clear photo of each nail next to a 10p, 20p, or 50p coin and upload it using the form below.

How to Measure

  • Place your finger on a flat surface.
  • Put a 10p, 20p, or 50p coin next to the nail.
  • Make sure both the nail and coin are visible.
  • Take a clear photo (one photo per hand is fine).
  • Upload the photos using the form below.
